Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Drop us an email and we'll be happy to help.

Where can I buy Cutie Cuddle products?

+
Cutie Cuddle products are available at major retailers across the United States, both in-store and online: TJ Maxx, Ross, Target, Amazon, Dick's Sporting Goods, Menards, and Kohl's. Look for us in the baby and children's departments. Availability may vary by location.

Are your products safe for newborns?

+
Absolutely. All Cutie Cuddle products are made with OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified materials, meaning they've been independently tested and confirmed free of harmful substances. Our fabrics are hypoallergenic, dye-tested, and formulated specifically for the sensitivity of newborn skin. We carry products for newborns (premie included) through 24 months.

What does "naturally fire resistant" mean?

+
Our fire resistance is achieved in two natural ways: first, by using inherently fire-resistant fibers like merino wool, which is naturally slow to ignite and self-extinguishing. Second, for our cotton-based sleepwear, we apply a plant-derived, formaldehyde-free treatment that meets CPSC 16 CFR Part 1615/1616 standards. We never use synthetic flame retardant chemicals, halogenated compounds, or PFAS.

How should I wash Cutie Cuddle items?

+
Most Cutie Cuddle items can be machine washed in cold water on a gentle cycle and tumble dried on low heat. We recommend washing in a mesh laundry bag for delicate items. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners — these can compromise the natural fire-resistant properties of the fabric. Always check the care label on each individual garment for specific instructions.

What sizes do you carry?

+
Our sizing range covers Premie, Newborn, 0–3M, 3–6M, 6–9M, 9–12M, 12–18M, and 18–24M. Sizing varies slightly by product line. Each product listing includes a detailed size chart. If you're unsure, we recommend sizing up — our garments are cut generously to allow for movement and layering.

Are your materials certified organic?

+
Yes. Our cotton is GOTS-certified (Global Organic Textile Standard), which is the world's leading standard for organic textile processing. This certification covers the entire supply chain — from raw fiber to the finished garment — ensuring no synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or prohibited dyes are used at any stage. Certification documentation is available upon request.

Do you ship internationally?

+
Cutie Cuddle products are currently distributed through U.S. retailers. International customers may be able to purchase through Amazon with international shipping enabled. We are actively working to expand our global distribution footprint — sign up for our newsletter to be among the first to know when we launch internationally.

How can I become a retail partner?

+
We'd love to explore a partnership! Please reach out to our wholesale and retail team directly at partners@cutiecuddle.com. Include information about your store, estimated order volume, and the markets you serve. Our team typically responds within 2 business days with our wholesale catalog and terms.
