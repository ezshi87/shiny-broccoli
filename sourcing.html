Transparency & Responsibility

What We're Made Of

Every fiber we choose, every supplier we partner with, and every garment we produce is guided by one simple principle: your baby deserves the very best nature has to offer.

Our Philosophy

Nature Knows Best

Long before synthetic fabrics existed, generations of parents wrapped their children in natural fibers — and for good reason. Natural materials breathe better, regulate temperature more effectively, and are inherently gentler on sensitive skin.

At Cutie Cuddle, we've taken that timeless wisdom and combined it with modern science to create baby apparel that is not only beautifully soft but also rigorously safe. Our fire-resistant properties are achieved through natural material selection and plant-based treatments — never through harsh chemicals or synthetic coatings.

We partner only with suppliers who meet our strict ethical and environmental standards, and we audit our supply chain regularly to ensure accountability at every level.

What We Use

Our Core Materials

Each material is chosen for a specific reason — and together they form a collection that is soft, safe, sustainable, and beautiful.

🌾

Certified Organic Cotton

Our primary fabric is 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton, grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. It's breathable, hypoallergenic, and gets softer with every wash — making it ideal for newborns and sensitive skin.

GOTS Certified Hypoallergenic Breathable
🎋

Bamboo Fiber

Bamboo is one of the most sustainable plants on earth — fast-growing, requiring no pesticides, and naturally antibacterial. Our bamboo viscose is extraordinarily soft (3x softer than cotton), moisture-wicking, and thermoregulating, keeping babies cool in summer and warm in winter.

Antibacterial Moisture-Wicking Thermoregulating
🐑

Merino Wool

Fine-grade merino wool is nature's perfect fiber for sleepwear. Unlike coarser wool, merino is soft enough for baby skin, naturally temperature-regulating, and inherently fire-resistant — meeting CPSC standards without any additional chemical treatment. We source only mulesing-free merino.

Naturally Fire Resistant Temperature Regulating Mulesing-Free
🌱

Natural Fire-Resistant Treatment

For our cotton-based sleepwear that requires added flame resistance, we use a plant-derived treatment formulated from naturally occurring compounds — free of formaldehyde, heavy metals, and phthalates. All treatments are independently tested to CPSC 16 CFR Part 1615/1616 standards.

CPSC Compliant No Formaldehyde Plant-Derived
How We Do It

Our Sourcing Process

From field to fabric to finished garment — every step is intentional, transparent, and held to the highest standard.

🔍
1

Ethical Supplier Vetting

We evaluate every potential supplier against our Code of Conduct — covering fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental impact.

🧪

Material Testing

Every fabric batch is independently tested by third-party labs for harmful substances, tensile strength, shrinkage, colorfastness, and fire safety compliance.

Responsible Manufacturing

Our manufacturing partners are WRAP and SA8000 certified. We conduct annual factory audits and maintain long-term relationships built on trust.

Quality Assurance

Before any product reaches a retailer shelf, it passes our in-house QA inspection — checking every seam, snap, and safety label.
